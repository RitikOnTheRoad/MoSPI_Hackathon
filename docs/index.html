📊 Analysis of Household Consumption and Expenditure Patterns in India

This project explores household expenditure, food consumption, and demographic trends across India. The study provides insights into how different family sizes, economic classes, and regions impact spending patterns and essential food consumption.

The visualizations cover MPCE trends, rice & pulses consumption, family size distribution, and statewise sex ratio. 📉📈 These insights can be useful for policymakers, researchers, and economists studying India's economic diversity.

💼 Employment Distribution Across States. 🚻 Statewise Sex Ratio and Demographic Trends 💰 Monthly Per Capita Expenditure (MPCE) 📈 Average MPCE for Different Family Sizes 🏠 Family Size Distribution Across Economic Classes 🍚 Monthly Consumption (₹) of Rice and Pulses 🌾 Rice and wheat Consumption Trends Across States

📌 Conclusion: The data highlights regional disparities in consumption and spending, helping us understand the economic behavior of Indian households.
